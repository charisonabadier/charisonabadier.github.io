About Me

I’m an MRI Radiographer pivoting into Data Analytics after more than a decade working in high‑precision, high‑accountability clinical environments. Since 2014, I’ve built a career on accuracy, critical thinking, and clear communication — the same strengths I now apply to analysing data, uncovering patterns, and translating insights into decisions that matter.

My move into analytics is driven by curiosity, challenge, and a desire to create impact beyond the clinical setting. I’m developing strong skills in Python, SQL, Power BI, and data storytelling, and I’m motivated by roles where data is used to improve processes, enhance user experience, and support strategic decision‑making.

I bring a unique blend of analytical discipline, real‑world problem‑solving, and the ability to communicate insights clearly to technical and non‑technical audiences. My goal is simple: turn complex data into meaningful, actionable intelligence that helps organisations move forward with confidence.
